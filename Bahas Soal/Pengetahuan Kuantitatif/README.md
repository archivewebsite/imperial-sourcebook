# Pengetahuan Kuantitatif

`Pengetahuan Kuantitatif` is the quantitative reasoning branch of `Bahas Soal`.

## What Belongs Here

- worked quantitative practice sets
- formulas and explanations tied to quantitative problem solving
- subfolders that group specific batches of worked examples

## What Does Not

- broad math theory notes that belong in `Resources/Notes` or `The Academy`

## How To Use It

- keep examples grouped by topic or exercise batch
- link to formula summaries when the same methods repeat
