# Motivation

`Motivation` stores notes that help restore perspective, momentum, or willingness to continue.

## What Belongs Here

- short perspective notes
- reminders worth rereading when energy drops
- material such as `Why Ambitious People Self-Destruct` when the point is behavioral reset

## What Does Not

- deep reflective journaling that belongs in `D:\Imperial Records\Areas`
- active plans or commitments that belong in `D:\Imperial Records`
- durable theory that belongs in `Notes` or `Learning & Skills`

## How To Use It

- keep notes rereadable under low motivation
- prefer concrete reminders over abstract hype
