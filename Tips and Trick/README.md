# Tips and Trick

`Tips and Trick` stores quick practical notes and tactical reminders.

## What Belongs Here

- short, high-utility notes such as `Why Ambitious People Self-Destruct`
- compact reminders that do not need a larger home yet

## What Does Not

- full essays that deserve a stronger taxonomy elsewhere

## How To Use It

- keep notes short, memorable, and easy to scan
- move clusters of related tips into a dedicated folder once they stop being miscellaneous
