## Too many men would pass the rape academy eye test

You probably heard that CNN just exposed an [online rape academy](https://www.cnn.com/interactive/2026/03/world/expose-rape-assault-online-vis-intl/index.html). It was married men, sharing tips like which drugs to use so she won’t realize. Some made videos. Some sold the videos to each other.

Dude, twenty bucks and you can watch me rape my unconscious wife.

==The site got 62 million views in February alone. Know who the users were? American men. I live in Canada, where the population is 41 million.== Y’all? That site got more views in a month than the population of my country.

It’s like men heard about the Gisèle Pelicot case and it sounded fun?

In the article, they talked to three of the wives it happened to.

Most didn’t want to talk, because my god, how do you deal with that? They were utterly broken. That was their best friend, their life partner, the man they felt safe sleeping beside. The father of their children.

Drugging them. Raping them. Selling videos.

Of the three women who shared their stories, two men are in jail. The third shot himself before trial. How do you tell his kids? How is she supposed to tell her mother-in-law? Will she say how was it rape, when he is your husband? Or will she be horrified at her own son?

One of the women found the videos. Took them to the police. Know what they said? That’s not evidence, it looks like you’re faking sleep.

The story of the rape academy broke the same week Andrew Tate hit bestseller status over on Substack. Do I need to tell you a lot of women are feeling pretty bruised?

When my friend’s husband put his fist through the wall right next to her head, I hugged her and said they’re not all like that, Becks. She said their little girl was watching and she was so scared she wet herself.

I looked at the bruises on her arm and said you deserve better. Not all men punch holes in the walls and manhandle their wives. My brother doesn’t. My uncle would never do that. Not all men. I said that. Me. But it’s true.

Do you know why women get mad when men say not all men? Because it’s making it about them. I mean, think about it. If a neighbor says my house got broken into, do we say not all men? Do we say I didn’t do it? No. I’m so sorry that happened is all that needs to be said. It’s all about context.

But still. The more technology changes, the more I see men using it to hurt women and I can’t unsee it. Hear me out. See if you agree.

The first time I wrote a feminist post, rape was happening about every two and a half minutes in America. Today, it’s every [68 seconds](https://rainn.org/statistics/scope-problem#:~:text=Every%2068%20seconds%20another%20American%20is%20sexually%20assaulted.&text=1%20out%20of%20every%206,completed%2C%202.8%25%20attempted\).&text=About%203%25%20of%20American%20men,completed%20rape%20in%20their%20lifetime.). 1,270 rapes per day. And you might think it has nothing to do with technology, but it does.

Every [nine minutes](https://rainn.org/get-informed/facts-statistics-the-scope-of-the-problem/), the rape is a child. According to [RAIIN](https://rainn.org/get-informed/facts-statistics-the-scope-of-the-problem/), whose stats come from the USA government, 82% of child victims are girls. ==And no, they aren’t sicko family members. Only 27% are related to the child.==

[Every 9 seconds](https://domesticviolencestatistics.org/domestic-violence-statistics/) a woman is assaulted or beaten. On a typical day, there are [20,800 calls](https://safehomesdv.org/about/domestic-violence-statistics/#:~:text=On%20a%20typical%20day%2C%20domestic,15%25%20of%20all%20violent%20crime.) to domestic abuse centers in America. Every year, [4.8 million](https://now.org/resource/violence-against-women-in-the-united-states-statistic/) partner-related physical assaults.

Every day, [3 women are murdered](https://www.huffingtonpost.ca/entry/domestic-violence-statistics_n_5959776) by their partner or ex-partner

From 2001–2012, America lost 6,488 Troops in [Afghanistan and Iraq](https://www.huffingtonpost.ca/entry/domestic-violence-statistics_n_5959776). In the same time frame, 11,766 women were killed by their intimate partners.

You’ve heard of the manosphere, right?

You know algorithms [funnel men](https://medium.com/fourth-wave/how-young-men-are-being-manipulated-into-hating-women-004fe24973f2) to that content, right? Because hate gets clicks. Indoctrination for views and engagement. It’s vile, but it happens.

Since the rise of the manosphere, there’s a new classification of murder. Pamela Edwards, of the National Resource Center For Domestic Violence did a [CBS interview](https://www.cbsnews.com/newyork/video/rejection-killings-dangers-women-face-when-telling-men-no/) about it. It’s called rejection killing.

Last year, two nineteen year old twins were out shopping when a young man asked them out. On the street in Brooklyn, New York. They said no, so [he stabbed one of them to death](https://www.cbsnews.com/newyork/video/rejection-killings-dangers-women-face-when-telling-men-no/) right there in the street.

Caroline Nosal, killed in a parking lot because a guy at work asked her out and she said no. Janese Jackson, [shot leaving a bar](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/janese-jackson-talton-shot-dead-after-rejecting-mans-advances-at-bar-documents-say/) because a guy asked her out and she said no. Andrea Farrington went to a mall and a security guard asked her out. She said no thanks. He [shot her three times](https://www.press-citizen.com/story/news/crime-and-courts/2016/06/06/kozak-sentenced-life-prison-without-parole/85259944/) in the back.

You don’t need me to keep going, right? Because I could.

It feels like what starts as an algorithm pushing content for views and engagement turns into hate and abuse that bleeds into the real world.

You know what happens when a woman reports a rape? Mostly nothing.

According to [Congress](https://crsreports.congress.gov/product/pdf/R/R44237/5) up to 400,000 rape kits sit unprocessed at any given time. Know why? The [Justice Dept](https://www.justice.gov/ovw/page/file/931391/dl?inline=). said it’s victim-blaming and bias against women. The men in charge of processing the kits don’t believe them so they just don’t process the kits. Are those the ‘good’ men?

Know what’s worse? Most men who rape children won’t see a courtroom. ==Anyone accused of a crime in America has a right to defend themselves, so the child must agree to be cross examined by the rapist’s attorney. Otherwise? Case closed.==

According to US government statistics, 60% of rapes are committed by someone known to the victim and [98%](https://rainn.org/facts-statistics-the-scope-of-the-problem/statistics-the-criminal-justice-system/) of reported rapists walk free.

I read [a piece](https://allyhamilton.yogisanonymous.com/p/prince-harming) by a woman who went to meet a man she’d been talking to online. Because despite this f — ed up world, some women still believe there’s a good man out there who will love and cherish them.

She got there first and while she was waiting, some other guy was hitting on her. Her date texted to say he’s almost there, and she looked away for a split second. Suddenly she was dizzy, head spinning and she realized he’d slipped something in her drink. Texted a friend. Please, come get me.

The guy she was supposed to meet got there just in time to help her into her friend’s car. Pleaded with her friend, please stay overnight with her.

It was a powerful read. She called it [Prince Harming](https://allyhamilton.yogisanonymous.com/p/prince-harming) and I wonder if all the “good men” realize how often this sort of thing happens to women.

==Why else would women have invented drink condoms you have to peel off to have a sip? Nail polish that changes color if someone slips date rape drugs into your drink? That’s the world women are living in now.==

Sometimes I’m stunned that women even use dating sites. Because vitriol, insults and dick pics. No man thinks a dick pic will get him a date. That’s not what it’s about. It’s power. Another way of saying I can do this to you. It’s like the # *yourbodymychoice* hashtag that went viral on Twitter.

Know what almost breaks me? We’re not even safe on the internet. I grew up in a world where women knew not to go out alone at night. Not to walk in the dark. Stay home, you’re safe at home, right. Sorry, not anymore.

I wrote about [nudify websites](https://medium.com/history-of-women/ai-undresses-women-and-girls-for-fun-makes-36-million-in-profit-967f00a3da9a) that let users take any photo and use AI to “undress” her. AI was trained on billions of explicit and porn images. Give AI a fully dressed photo, it has a darn good idea what any woman or girl looks like under her clothing. Women who’ve been nudified say they’re stunningly accurate. 90% of nudified images are of women and girls.

One woman said someone took photos from when she was pregnant and turned them into porn. How do you undo the pain of seeing that?

I wrote about a [spicy AI site](https://medium.com/ai-ai-oh/spicy-ai-site-leaked-millions-of-files-and-its-as-bad-as-you-d-think-bfd1cd7bdb5b?sk=bb5fb1df74d20d89d253776e743e6dc6) that leaked millions of images. The site lets men create “spicy” AI girlfriends that perform on command. Wink wink. Know what men were doing? Creating ‘spicy’ AI girlfriends from photos of actual women without their consent. “Performing” on command.

I read about a woman who found a man running an OnlyFans pretending to be her. Using AI to create nudes and videos of her and selling access. She got him shut down, but it’s like whack-a-mole. She was in tears, saying she doesn’t know how to make it stop. He shares photos on social media to drive traffic to the OnlyFans and sometimes her friends see them.

Can I interrupt myself to say it’s not just that? If all that (\*flaps hands\*) isn’t enough, we still don’t get equal pay.

Women earn 82 cents on the male dollar and while that might seem like whining about pennies, it works out to around [$10,000 less](https://www.americanprogress.org/issues/women/reports/2020/03/24/482141/quick-facts-gender-wage-gap/#:~:text=The%20gender%20wage%20gap%20is%20more%20than%20just%20a%20few%20cents&text=An%20even%20larger%20consideration%20is,their%20male%20counterparts%20in%202019.) per year and half a million less over the course of an entire 40 year career.

So if we do pair up, we go to work and earn less than a man for the same job and come home to do [most of the housework](https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/women-housework-division-labour-1.7426763) because too many men still think housework is woman’s work. Did you know only [29%](https://www.americanprogress.org/issues/women/reports/2019/05/10/469739/breadwinning-mothers-continue-u-s-norm/) of American households exist on the man’s salary alone today?

We go into the world and deal with random men who cop a feel, brush a hand across our chest or butt, harass women in bars or catcall in the streets and walk across the parking lot with our keys laced through our fingers.

To get home and do chores while he watches tv or plays a video game and if he does help he needs to be asked and you wonder why 72% of divorces are initiated by the woman? It’s just too much, y’all. Too much.

==The same week the rape academy story broke, Former House Speaker Kevin McCarthy went on ABC and said every Congress member knew not to let young staffers alone around Swalwell.== Same story we heard during the Weinstein trial, and the Cosby trial. And Louis C.K. I’m still mad about him because he was funny. Why is this always hidden behind whispers?

Why aren’t more men saying this stuff out loud? ==Why do so many men shrug off locker room talk?== Why did all those Olympic hockey players laugh when Trump said we “have to” invite the women? Are those good men?

And if they had daughters, how did they feel to see him laugh?

You know what was trending on social media a month or so ago? Alpine divorce. Now it’s maritime. Men going online to talk about abandoning women on hiking trails and mountain tops and laughing about it.

Knowing all that, you know what undoes me? The number of women who still somehow believe there’s a man out there who’ll be her safe harbor in this vile, ugly world. A good man who will love her and not hurt her.

==A man who can see her as an equal partner.== Admire a beautiful woman without turning her into porn. Go on a hike and not abandon her in the forest. Get married and not slip drugs in her food to secretly rape her.

Those women? They’re not wrong. ==There are good men in the world.==

==According to== ==[Pew Research](https://www.pewresearch.org/social-trends/2020/07/07/a-century-after-women-gained-the-right-to-vote-majority-of-americans-see-work-to-do-on-gender-equality/)====, seventy nine percent of Americans believe men and women are equal partners== and I hang onto that number. But I wonder if the good men realize they *feel like* the minority. It’s hard to feel like good men are the majority when so many stay silent, you know?

It makes me think of the most popular videos on the rape academy. Know what they were? Not the rapes. The eye test.

After men drugged their wives, they’d lean over and open her eyelid. They’d make videos. See? Her eye is open but she sees nothing. Sometimes, that feels like too much of the world. Your eyes are open. But can you see?
