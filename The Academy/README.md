# The Academy

`The Academy` is the structured academic branch of the vault. Use it for formal study material, exam-adjacent planning, and subject-based training systems.

## Current Buckets

- `Admissions & Applications`: application and entry-path material.
- `Core Subjects`: the main academic skill domains.
- `Study Management`: how the study system is run.
- `The Anthology`: curated readings or collected texts.

## What Belongs Here

- academic systems and subject training
- formal study notes that benefit from explicit curriculum-like structure
- notes that are easier to navigate by academic role than by generic resource taxonomy

## What Does Not

- broad personal knowledge notes that belong elsewhere in `Resources`
- active project work that belongs in `Projects`

## How To Use It

- keep the structure explicit enough to support repeated study
- let child folders carry the subject-specific detail
