# Design Inspiration

`Design Inspiration` stores references worth revisiting for visual, interaction, layout, or product ideas.

## What Belongs Here

- saved design references
- notes such as `Design`
- examples you may want to borrow from later

## What Does Not

- active design project work that belongs in `Projects`
- evergreen design theory that belongs in `Notes` or `Personal Wikipedia`

## How To Use It

- save the reference with the reason it matters
- tag or title notes by the aspect worth borrowing, not just the source
