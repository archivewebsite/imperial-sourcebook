Treat this phase as a temporary superpower: pour every hour into building real things that solve painful problems for paying users, validate obsessively in public forums before polishing, and charge early to force market truth, all while protecting the machine (sleep, minimal nutrition, one non-negotiable recovery ritual) so you don't flame out before momentum hits. The data proves this archetype already generates real wins—$10k–$100k solo revenue streams in niches—but only when grit meets iteration, when you layer basic distribution and taste onto the tech, and when you stay obsessed with customers rather than just output speed. Don't romanticize the struggle forever; use this window to stack small victories, network sideways through the communities you're already shipping into, and build habits that compound into freedom later. The window is open now because the barriers fell, but it closes when life fills the calendar—ship ugly, learn fast, and own the hunger while it lasts.
