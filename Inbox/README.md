# Inbox

`Inbox` is Sourcebook's temporary intake folder for material that is already meant to become reference, study, or knowledge material.

## What Belongs Here

- rough study notes that are not ready to file
- knowledge fragments copied into Sourcebook before their final home is clear
- source-derived notes that need a quick cleanup before moving to `Notes`, `Learning & Skills`, `Bahas Soal`, or another Sourcebook bucket

## What Does Not

- general rough capture that belongs in `D:\Imperial Records\Inbox`
- active project plans that belong in `D:\Imperial Records\Projects`
- future commitments that belong in `D:\Imperial Records\Labs\Idea Backlog`
- imported articles that should stay in `Clippings` until cleaned

## How To Use It

- keep capture fast, but only for material that belongs in this knowledge vault
- rename vague files before moving them into a permanent folder
- route notes out when their role is clear
- leave operational context in Records and keep only reusable reference material here

## Review Rhythm

Review this folder whenever it starts hiding useful study or reference notes. It should stay small enough that triage is easy.
