# Random Questions

This folder stores questions that pop into your head and feel worth keeping, even when you do not have an answer yet.

## Rules

- Keep one question per note.
- Use the question itself as the note title and H1.
- Use a Windows-safe filename when the question includes characters such as `?`.
- Add thoughts, links, or partial answers to the same note over time instead of starting over elsewhere.

## What Belongs Here

- open-ended curiosities
- questions worth researching later
- prompts that may grow into concept notes

## What Does Not

- future projects or goals that belong in `Resources/Idea Backlog`
- raw capture that should stay in `Inbox`
- finished evergreen explanations that belong in `Resources/Personal Wikipedia`
