# Bahas Soal

`Bahas Soal` is for worked solutions, breakdowns, and formula-based notes tied to practice problems.

## What Belongs Here

- solved examples and step-by-step breakdowns
- formula notes that directly support problem solving
- practice-oriented material such as `Konfigurasi Elektron Pengertian Aturan Contoh Soal Dan Pembahasannya Lengkap`

## What Does Not

- broad theory notes that belong in `Notes`
- active project work that belongs in `D:\Imperial Records\Projects`

## How To Use It

- keep the solution path visible, not just the final answer
- group related material by domain when the folder starts growing
- move reusable theory into `Notes` or `Learning & Skills` when it stops being practice support
