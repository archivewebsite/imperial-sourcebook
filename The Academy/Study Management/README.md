# Study Management

`Study Management` stores the systems that make studying repeatable rather than improvised.

## What Belongs Here

- notes such as `MIT Mindset, Impostor Syndrome, and How Learning Works`
- scheduling, review cadence, and meta-study guidance

## What Does Not

- subject-specific notes that belong in the relevant academic folder

## How To Use It

- keep this folder focused on how you study, not what you study
