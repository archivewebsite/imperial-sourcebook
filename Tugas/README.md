# Tugas

`Tugas` stores assignment outputs and school-task material that are worth preserving as reference after the immediate task is done.

## Current Buckets

- `Bahasa Indonesia`: Indonesian-language assignment material and essay work.

## What Belongs Here

- completed or near-complete school assignments
- essay drafts or task outputs that can be studied later
- assignment material that connects to broader Sourcebook study notes

## What Does Not

- active task planning that belongs in `D:\Imperial Records\Projects`
- recurring school or life systems that belong in `D:\Imperial Records\Areas`
- rough capture that belongs in an Inbox
- general subject notes that belong in `Notes` or `Learning & Skills`

## How To Use It

- keep the assignment context visible enough to understand the note later
- move reusable theory or formulas into the relevant study folder when they outgrow the assignment
- keep deliverable planning in Records and durable output here
