# Curiosity Shelf

`Curiosity Shelf` holds books, topics, and references you want to keep visible precisely because they sit just beyond your current mastery.

## What Belongs Here

- unread or partially explored books worth revisiting later
- frontier topics you want to remain intellectually aware of
- references that still function as visible unknowns rather than finished study notes

## How To Use It

- use this folder as a visible edge-of-knowledge shelf, not as a general backlog
- keep entries lightweight until you actually study or rewrite them
- move material into `Learning & Skills`, `The Academy`, or `Personal Wikipedia` once it becomes active learning or durable reference
